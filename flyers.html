CLUB SCAM Flyer, Martin Guevara, 2018, Photoshop, poster

This CLUB SCAM flyer was commissioned by Josue Ramirez, who throws a monthly party for the LGBTQ+ community in San Jose which is a response to the lack of queer spaces in the South Bay.

Hardware Flyer, Martin Guevara, 2019, Photoshop, poster

Hardware Promo Video, Martin Guevara, 2019, video

In 2019, I started a party called Hardware SF with the help of friends. This party happens every three months and serves as a safe space for the LGBTQ+ communities from all over the Bay Area. The space in which we throw the party is kept a secret in the style of underground parties and raves of the 90's. In order to get the address to the party you have to direct message the hosts or wait for the day of to receive the address. From there you follow the pink arrows down two flights of stairs underneath the streets of Japantown. I was inspired by my older sister's stories of underground raves that would happen all over San Francisco in the 90's. Growing up I shared a room with her and I remember the walls being covered in flyers and posters. I suppose this party is a manifestation of those memories.
